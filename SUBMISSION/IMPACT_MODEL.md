# S.O.M.I.S Impact Model: Quantifying "The Indian Investor" Advantage

## 1. Problem: The "Flying Blind" Cost
India has **14 crore+ demat accounts**, but **90%+ of retail investors** trade on "tips" or "1st-order reactions." 
*   **Time Loss:** A typical investor spends **4-6 hours** manually reading news, checking supply chain links, and correlating global events to Indian tickers.
*   **Performance Drag:** Reacting to "1st-order news" (e.g., selling on a bad headline) often leads to entering late or exiting early, missing the "true ripple."

## 2. Solution: S.O.M.I.S Efficiency
### A. Time Saved per User
*   **Traditional Process:** 5 hours (Research + Correlation + Verification)
*   **S.O.M.I.S Swarm:** ~10-30 seconds per news item.
*   **Efficiency Multiplier:** **~1,200x speed increase** in identifying actionable trade ideas.

### B. Scalable Alpha Generation (Alpha-Graph)
By identifying **Second-Order Impacts** (e.g., if a war in the Middle East impacts global logistics, S.O.M.I.S identifies Indian port operators or logistics companies), investors gain a "Front-Running" advantage on the market cycle.
*   **Hypothetical Alpha:** Catching a 2nd-order ripple just **2 hours** before the broader market reacts can capture an additional **2-4%** in move potential per event.

### C. Zero API Cost for Retail (Democratic Access)
Traditional institutional tools (Bloomberg, Reuters Eikon) cost **$2,000+/month**.
*   **S.O.M.I.S Model:** Runs on consumer hardware with local LLMs (Phi-3.5).
*   **Impact:** Direct democratization of Institutional-grade research tools to the masses for **₹0 monthly API costs**.

## 3. The Math (Back-of-the-Envelope)
*   **Target:** 1,000,000 active Indian Retail Users.
*   **Daily Time Saved:** 1,000,000 users * 1 hour/day = **1,000,000 hours/day saved** for the Indian economy.
*   **Information Edge:** Automating the "Reasoning Chain" reduces emotional trading, potentially saving retail investors **crores of rupees** in bad "gut-feel" trades.
